Activities

Volunteering with or becoming a member of Celbridge Community Council is a great way for Celbridge residents to increase their involvement in their own community. Celbridge Community Council supports and promotes many different community based activities. New members from many backgrounds and with many different skills are always welcome to participate in any way they can. Volunteers and members who currently assist in our activities do so in a number of different ways including the following:

Care for the Aged

Celbridge Community Council has a long tradition of providing Care for the Aged in our community. If you would like to be involved in this very valuable work or enquire about any of our services, please get in touch.

You can email us on info@celbridgecommunitycouncil.ie or call us on (01) 630 3599 (phones are answered from 9am-1pm on Mondays and Thursdays but you can leave a voice mail message at any time).

Personal Alarms

Celbridge Community Council has administered the Seniors Alert Scheme for many years. This scheme supports vulnerable older people in our community through the provision of personal monitored alarms to enable older persons, to continue to live securely in their homes with confidence, independence and peace of mind.

Find out more

Meals on Wheels

Celbridge Meals on Wheels delivers meals in the locality on a twice weekly basis. Recipients benefit through the improvement of their nutritional status, health and quality of life but also through the provision of a source of social contact and connectivity.

Celbridge Community Council supports this valuable work and tries to minimise the cost of these meals to recipients by obtaining funding from the HSE and by contributing to the shortfall in funding.
